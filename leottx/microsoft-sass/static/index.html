São Paulo

Brazil

Recognized as one of the best places to work in Brazil, the São Paulo office houses teams in engineering, sales, support, finance, human resources, development, and marketing. Located within the United Nations Business Center, and overlooking the Estaiada Bridge, employees are within walking-distance of this festive city’s hotels, banks, restaurants, cafés, and popular shops. Renovated office spaces offer an attached medical complex, easy parking, foosball tables, video games for Xbox, a wealth of snacks, and the Microsoft Technology Center, where employees can come together for colorful celebrations Brazil style.
